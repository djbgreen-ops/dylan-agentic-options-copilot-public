# Safety and Reliability

D.Y.L.A.N. uses safety boundaries designed for supervised decision support.

## Controls

- human approval required
- no live capital approval
- no market orders
- deterministic risk checks
- final validation
- disclosure gates
- no-overstatement scans
- status-floor checks
- trace and journal evidence

## Status floor

production_candidate / backtest_required / paper_trade_required / live_capital_not_approved

## Human oversight

The system is a co-pilot. It does not replace the human operator.
